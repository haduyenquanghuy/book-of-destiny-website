Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Book Of Destiny is designed as a simple entertainment and reflection app. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

2. Questions You Enter

The questions you type in the app are used only to provide the app experience. They are not used to personally identify you.

Book Of Destiny provides random pre-written answers for entertainment and self-reflection purposes. The answers should not be considered professional, legal, medical, financial, or psychological advice.

4. Third-Party Services

At this time, Book Of Destiny does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Book Of Destiny is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

Since the app does not require account registration or collect personal information, we do not store personal user data on our servers.
